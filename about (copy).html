Our Creative Team

Arash G. NasrabadiCEO & MM

Arash is graduated in International Marketing and Innovation Management with a 2nd Level Master at Scuola Superiore Sant’Anna. He is expert and passionate of startups mentorship, especially regarding financial aspects, crowdfunding and business development. He collaborated with Corporate Venture Capital of Telecom Italia and had been part of a Fulbright program in Silicon Valley (Santa Clara University).

Alessio BalsiniVP & Developer

Alessio is graduated in Computer Engineering (University of Pisa) and Embedded Computing Systems (Scuola Superiore Sant’Anna) cum laude. His fields of interest are especially related to low-level programming and electronics. Alessandro BiondiTech. Manager

Alessandro is graduated cum laude in Computer Engineering at University of Pisa, within the excellence program. Currently, he is a PhD Fellow at the Real-Time Systems (ReTiS) Laboratory of Scuola Superiore Sant’Anna. He has been awarded multiple times by the Cyber-Physical Systems and Real-Time Systems research communities. He has been visiting researcher at Max Planck Institute for Software Systems.

Davide CalvaresiProj. Manager

Davide is graduated in Computer and Automation Engineering (Università Politecnica delle Marche, 2014).
Currently, he is Ph.D. student at Scuola Superiore Sant’Anna (ReTiS Lab), and he is research assistant at the University of Applied Sciences Western Switzerland. His research interests focus on Assistive Environments and Technologies empowered by the radically new Real-Time Multi-Agents Systems (his major contribution).

Giorgio ButtazzoAdvisor

Giorgio is Full Professor of Computer Engineering at the Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna of Pisa. He worked on active perception and real-time control at the G.R.A.S.P. Laboratory of the University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia. In 2003, he was Co-Founder of Evidence s.r.l., a spin-off company of the Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna providing software solutions for real-time embedded systems. He has authored 6 books on real-time systems and over 200 papers in the field of real-time systems, robotics, and neural networks.
